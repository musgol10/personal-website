Case Studies

On-Premise Generative AI Assistant

Problem: Employees lacked a secure, centralized way to query internal knowledge bases, leading to lost productivity and reliance on siloed information.

Solution: Led the product strategy and development of a fully on-premise AI assistant. The system used RAG to securely access internal documents and integrated with company tools. Deployed with a privacy-first architecture to meet corporate compliance.

Impact: Reduced query time for complex internal questions by an estimated 70% in pilot groups. Enhanced data security by keeping all sensitive information within the company's firewall.

RAG for Customer Support Knowledge Base

Problem: Support agents struggled to find accurate answers quickly, leading to longer resolution times and inconsistent service. Knowledge was split between a leading CRM platform and various internal documents.

Solution: Developed and deployed a RAG application that unified structured CRM data and unstructured documents into a single, searchable interface. The assistant provided agents with instant, context-aware answers.

Impact: Significantly decreased average call handling time and markedly improved first-call resolution rates, boosting agent confidence and customer satisfaction.
